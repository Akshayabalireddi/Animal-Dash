About Us

We are a team of four interns- Akshaya, Roisin, Hamza, and Yousif,Who developed Animal Dash during our one-month internship project. Animal Dash is an exciting runner game where players guide a animal each level through various challenges, avoiding obstacles and collecting rewards along the way. This project allowed us to strengthen our skills in programming, game design, problem-solving, and teamwork. We worked together throughout the development process, from planning and designing to coding and testing. We are proud to present Animal Dash as a reflection of our creativity, collaboration, and learning experience.

Our Characters

  • Character-1-DUCK

    The easiest level in Animal Dash. Collect bread, avoid obstacles, and learn the basics of the game as you begin your adventure.

  • Character-2-DEER

    A step up from Level 1. Collect berries, avoid obstacles, and test your speed and agility as the challenge increases.

  • Character-1-WHALE

    Enter a new aquatic environment and collect fish while navigating through exciting underwater challenges.

  • Character-4-LION

    Face your toughest challenge yet! Collect meat, avoid obstacles, and stay out of the hunter's sight as you race to survive.
